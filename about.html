Why Avanza ABA Was Built

Avanza ABA was founded to provide care that is more intentional, more consistent, and more connected to real life—so families can see meaningful progress where it matters most.

Who We Are

Rooted in care.
Guided by clinical excellence.

Avanza ABA is a clinician-founded practice built to deliver care that is intentional, responsive, and grounded in real life.

"Avanza," meaning to move forward, reflects our commitment to helping children and families make meaningful progress—at home, in school, and in everyday routines.

Every program is guided by clinical expertise and designed around the individual—not just the diagnosis—so progress is not only measured, but experienced in everyday life.

Working With Us

What to expect from working with us

From your first call through ongoing care, here is what you can always count on from our team.

Clear guidance from day one

We walk you through every step—from intake to ongoing services—so you always know what comes next.

Care that adapts as your child grows

Programs are continuously adjusted based on progress—not followed as a fixed plan.

Support beyond sessions

We focus on helping skills carry into daily routines, not just structured therapy time.

Consistency across environments

We align care across home, school, and community so progress doesn't stay in one place.

Communication you can rely on

You'll always know how your child is progressing and what we're working on next.

A team that stays engaged

Your child's care is actively guided—not passed off or left stagnant.

Our Team

Meet the team guiding your child's progress

Every member of our team is selected for clinical excellence, genuine care, and a commitment to meaningful outcomes.

Board Certified Behavior Analysts Bilingual support available In-home & community-based care Serving children, teens, and adults

Valerie Smith, M.Ed, BCBA, LBA

Founder & Clinical Director

Avanza ABA was founded by Valerie Smith, a Board Certified Behavior Analyst with extensive experience leading and developing large-scale ABA programs. Valerie provides direct clinical oversight, ensuring every client receives individualized, high-quality care rooted in both clinical excellence and real-life application. She is passionate about supporting families through collaboration, developing strong clinicians, and creating meaningful progress that extends beyond therapy sessions.

Stacy Luster, RBT

Lead Behavior Technician

Stacy plays a key role in delivering consistent, high-quality care and supporting the implementation of individualized treatment plans. She works closely with both families and clinical leadership to ensure sessions are engaging, effective, and aligned with each individual's goals. Known for her warm, approachable style, she helps create a supportive environment where meaningful progress can happen.

Our Approach to Care

Our approach to care

At Avanza ABA, our team is built on more than credentials—we prioritize clinical quality, collaboration, and consistency. Every team member receives ongoing training, supervision, and support to ensure care remains ethical, individualized, and effective.

We believe progress happens when strong clinical guidance meets real-life application—and that guides everything we do.

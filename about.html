Our Story

A practice born from purpose — rooted in community, driven by the belief that every individual deserves the chance to grow.

Who We Are

Rooted in care.
Growing with purpose.

Avanza ABA was founded on a simple but powerful belief: every individual — regardless of age, ability, or background — carries within them the capacity to advance, grow, and flourish.

Our name, Avanza, is Spanish for "to advance." It reflects our roots, our mission, and our promise to the families we serve across Arizona. We are a Center of Excellence for Autism — and we take that title seriously.

We are a team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts, Registered Behavior Technicians, and support professionals who bring both clinical expertise and genuine compassion to every session. We believe in treating the whole person — not just the diagnosis.

Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Our values are not just words — they are the foundation of every interaction, every plan, and every outcome we work toward together.

🌱

Family Centered

Families are the core of everything we do. We partner with parents, caregivers, and siblings as active members of the therapy team.

🔬

Evidence Based

Every intervention we use is grounded in Applied Behavior Analysis — the gold standard, most researched approach in autism care.

🤝

Culturally Inclusive

We honor every background, language, and family structure. Progress is universal — and so is our welcome.

🌎

Community Rooted

We are your neighbors. Our commitment extends beyond the therapy room — to the schools, neighborhoods, and communities we share.

✨

Excellence Driven

We hold ourselves to the highest clinical and ethical standards — because your family deserves nothing less than the best.

💛

Autism Friendly

Our spaces, schedules, and approaches are designed with sensory needs in mind — warm, calm, and built for comfort.

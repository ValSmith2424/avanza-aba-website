Privacy Policy

How Avanza ABA collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: [Date to be added by Avanza ABA]

1. Information We Collect

Avanza ABA collects personal information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, complete intake forms, or interact with our website. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and health-related information necessary to provide behavioral health services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to schedule and provide ABA therapy services, communicate with you about your care, process insurance and billing, comply with legal and regulatory requirements, and improve our website and services.

3. Health Information & Privacy

Protected health information (PHI) is handled in accordance with all applicable federal and state laws. We do not sell or share your PHI with third parties except as required to provide your care or as required by law.

4. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect anonymized data and do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted forms and secure data storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our contact page or by calling our office directly.

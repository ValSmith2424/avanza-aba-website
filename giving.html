Giving Back

Avanza ABA's commitment extends beyond the therapy room — into the schools, communities, and families that make up the fabric of Arizona.

Our Commitment

Rooted in community

The roots in our logo aren't just symbolic — they represent our deep connection to the communities we serve. At Avanza ABA, giving back is not an afterthought. It's woven into who we are and how we operate.

We believe that access to behavioral health resources should not be determined by zip code, income, or language. That belief drives our outreach, education, and advocacy work across Arizona.

🌱
Initiatives

How we give back

🎓

Community Education

Free workshops, webinars, and school presentations on autism awareness, ABA basics, and behavioral strategies for educators and caregivers.

🤝

Provider Partnerships

Collaborating with pediatricians, schools, and social service organizations to connect families with the resources they need — regardless of their ability to pay.

🗣️

Autism Advocacy

Supporting autism awareness events, legislative advocacy for coverage rights, and community campaigns that reduce stigma and celebrate neurodiversity.

💛

Scholarship & Reduced-Fee Services

A commitment to providing reduced-fee services for families facing financial barriers — because access to care should not be a privilege.

🌎

Culturally Responsive Outreach

Spanish-language resources, bilingual staff, and community outreach designed to reach underserved Hispanic and Latino families across Arizona.

🏫

School Consultation

Pro-bono and reduced-cost consultation for Arizona schools to support IEP teams, classroom behavior support, and teacher training.

Partner With Us

Let's build something together

Whether you're a school, clinic, community organization, or advocacy group — we'd love to explore how we can work together to better serve Arizona families.

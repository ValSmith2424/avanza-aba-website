What to Expect

What's in the intake forms?

👤

Patient Information

Basic demographic information about the individual receiving services — name, DOB, address, emergency contacts.

💳

Insurance Details

Primary and secondary insurance information so we can verify your benefits before your first session.

🏥

Background & History

Medical history, current diagnoses, previous therapies, school information, and your goals for care.

✍️

Consents & Signatures

HIPAA acknowledgment, consent for treatment, financial agreement, and optional telehealth/photo consents — all signed digitally.

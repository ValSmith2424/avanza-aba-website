HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices

Your rights regarding your protected health information and how Avanza ABA uses it.

Last updated: [Date to be added by Avanza ABA]

Your Rights

As a patient of Avanza ABA, you have the right to:

  • Get a copy of your health and claims records
  • Correct your health and claims records
  • Request confidential communications
  • Ask us to limit the information we share
  • Get a list of those with whom we've shared your information
  • Get a copy of this privacy notice
  • Choose someone to act on your behalf
  • File a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

Our Uses and Disclosures

We typically use or share your health information in the following ways:

  • Treat you — We share information with other providers involved in your care.
  • Run our organization — We use and share your information to run our practice and improve your care.
  • Bill for your services — We share information about your care with insurance companies and others who pay for your services.

How We Protect Your Information

Avanza ABA is required by law to maintain the privacy and security of your protected health information. We will let you know promptly if a breach occurs that may have compromised the privacy or security of your information.

Contact & Complaints

For questions about this notice or to file a complaint, contact our Privacy Officer via our contact page. You may also file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights.

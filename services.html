Our Services

Individualized, evidence-based ABA therapy delivered where it matters most — at home, in clinic, at school, and everywhere in between.

In-Home

In-Home ABA Therapy

Home is where children feel safest and most themselves — which makes it the ideal setting for meaningful, lasting change. Our in-home ABA services bring evidence-based therapy directly to your family in a naturalistic, low-stress environment.

Sessions focus on real-life skill building: communication, daily living skills, social interactions, and reducing challenging behaviors in the context where they actually occur.

  • Naturalistic teaching

    Skills taught in real-life routines and environments for maximum generalization.

  • Parent coaching included

    We train caregivers to support and extend progress throughout the day.

  • Flexible scheduling

    Sessions designed around your family's schedule and daily routines.

🏠
School-Based

School-Based Support

We collaborate directly with schools, teachers, and IEP teams to provide behavior support and skill development in the educational setting. Our school-based services reinforce learning, social integration, and independence — right where children spend most of their day.

  • IEP collaboration

    We work alongside school teams to support and align with IEP goals.

  • Classroom support

    Behavior technicians embedded in the classroom for real-time support.

Family Guidance

Family Guidance

Parents and caregivers are the most powerful force in a child's progress. Our family guidance program empowers you with the strategies, knowledge, and confidence to support your child's growth every day — through real collaboration, not just instruction.

  • Collaboration & empowerment

    We work alongside you — not just with your child. Your insight shapes every goal and every session.

  • PFA/SBT approach

    Using Practical Functional Assessment and Skill-Based Training to address behavior through understanding, not just management.

  • Ongoing support

    Regular guidance sessions, resources, and a team that's always a call away.

Assessment

Behavioral Assessments

Every effective treatment plan begins with a thorough understanding of the individual. Our BCBAs conduct comprehensive behavioral evaluations to identify strengths, areas for growth, and the specific skills to target in therapy.

  • Standardized assessments

    Using validated tools to measure skills and behavior across domains.

  • Family-informed

    Your insight and priorities are central to every assessment.

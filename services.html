Our Services

Care designed around each individual's strengths, needs, and real-life goals—delivered with consistency and clinical oversight.

In-Home

In-Home ABA Therapy
That Works in Real Life

Our in-home ABA therapy is designed to go beyond structured sessions. We focus on helping children build skills they actually use every day—communication, independence, social interaction, and navigating real-life situations.

Every program is individualized and actively supervised by a BCBA, ensuring therapy is continuously adjusted based on progress, not just followed as a set plan.

  • Naturalistic Teaching

    Skills are taught during real routines—mealtimes, play, transitions—so they actually carry over.

  • Family Guidance Included

    You're not left guessing. We show you how to support progress throughout the day.

  • Flexible Scheduling

    Sessions are built around your schedule—not the other way around.

Why families choose in-home services with Avanza:

Therapy happens in natural environments
Parents are actively involved
Programs are supervised and adjusted regularly
Flexible scheduling
We are currently accepting new clients and can guide you through insurance and next steps quickly.
School-Based

School-Based ABA Support
That Carries Over

We help bridge the gap between therapy and the classroom—so your child can succeed where it matters most. Our support focuses on attention, independence, social interaction, and classroom participation.

We collaborate with school teams to ensure consistency across environments.

What this looks like:

Supporting classroom routines and expectations
Helping with peer interactions and social skills
Increasing independence with school tasks
Reducing behaviors that interfere with learning

Support that carries into the classroom—and beyond.

Community

Community-Based ABA Therapy
for Real-World Success

Skills don't just need to work at home—they need to work everywhere. Our community-based services focus on helping individuals navigate real-life environments like parks, stores, and social settings with confidence and independence.

What this looks like:

Practicing social skills in community environments
Building independence in everyday activities
Supporting transitions and flexibility in public settings
Applying communication skills in real-life situations

Helping skills carry over into everyday life.

Family Guidance

Family Guidance That Supports
Lasting Progress

Progress doesn't stop when a session ends—and neither does our support. We work closely with caregivers to provide practical, easy-to-use strategies that help extend progress throughout the day.

What this looks like:

Coaching during real-life situations
Guidance on behavior and communication strategies
Support with routines, transitions, and expectations
Ongoing collaboration and feedback

Support for your child—and for you.

Assessment

Comprehensive Assessments
That Guide Meaningful Care

Every effective program starts with understanding. Our assessments identify strengths, needs, and meaningful goals—so your child's plan is intentional from the start.

What this includes:

Skill assessments across key areas
Behavior analysis and function-based planning
Individualized treatment recommendations
Ongoing progress review and updates

Start with a clear, thoughtful plan.

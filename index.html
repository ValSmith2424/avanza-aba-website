Who We Are

Rooted in care.
Guided by clinical excellence.

Avanza ABA is a clinician-founded practice built to deliver care that is intentional, responsive, and grounded in real life. "Avanza," meaning to move forward, reflects our commitment to helping children and families make meaningful progress—at home, in school, and in everyday routines.

Every program is guided by clinical expertise and designed around the individual—not just the diagnosis—so progress is not only measured, but experienced in everyday life.

Clinical Excellence

BCBA-founded and led, with direct clinical oversight guiding every program.

Family Partnership

You're not on the outside looking in—we work alongside you as partners in your child's progress.

Culturally Attuned

Care delivered in a way that feels natural to your family—bilingual, inclusive, and respectful.

Real-Life Focus

Skills built for everyday life—at home, in school, and in the community.

How It Works

Your path to progress

Getting started with Avanza ABA is simple. We guide you every step of the way — from your first call through active therapy and beyond.

1

Free Consultation

Connect with our team. We listen, answer questions, and learn about your family's goals — no pressure, no commitment.

2

Insurance Verification

We verify your benefits, explain coverage, and handle authorization — at no cost to you.

3

Behavioral Assessment

A BCBA conducts a comprehensive evaluation to understand your child's unique strengths and areas for growth.

4

Personalized Plan

We build an individualized treatment plan collaboratively with your family — with clear, measurable goals.

5

Therapy Begins

Sessions start in your preferred setting — home or school — with continuous progress monitoring.

Who We Are

Rooted in family.
Built for progress.

Avanza ABA is an Arizona-based Center of Excellence for Autism, offering Applied Behavior Analysis therapy that is warm, individualized, and deeply family-centered. Our name — Spanish for "to advance" — reflects our mission: every individual, every family, moving forward.

Warm & Welcoming

A practice where every family feels seen, heard, and genuinely supported from day one.

Evidence-Based Practice

Every intervention grounded in Applied Behavior Analysis — proven, ethical, individualized.

Culturally Inclusive

Honoring every background, language, and family structure — because progress is universal.

Community Rooted

We are your neighbors — committed to Arizona families beyond the therapy room.

How It Works

Your path to progress

Getting started with Avanza ABA is simple. We guide you every step of the way — from your first call through active therapy and beyond.

1

Free Consultation

Connect with our team. We listen, answer questions, and learn about your family's goals — no pressure, no commitment.

2

Insurance Verification

We verify your benefits, explain coverage, and handle authorization — at no cost to you.

3

Behavioral Assessment

A BCBA conducts a comprehensive evaluation to understand your child's unique strengths and areas for growth.

4

Personalized Plan

We build an individualized treatment plan collaboratively with your family — with clear, measurable goals.

5

Therapy Begins

Sessions start in your preferred setting — home, clinic, or school — with continuous progress monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientifically validated approach to understanding behavior and how it's influenced by the environment. It's used to help individuals develop communication, social, and daily living skills while reducing challenging behaviors.
We serve children, adolescents, adults, and their families. Our programs are designed for individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD), developmental delays, and related behavioral health needs — across all age ranges.
Yes. We work with many major insurance providers including AHCCCS/Medicaid, Blue Cross Blue Shield of AZ, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and more. We verify your benefits before your first appointment at no cost to you.
We offer in-home, clinic-based, school-based, and telehealth services depending on your individualized treatment plan. Many families use a combination of settings for the best outcomes.

Who We Are

Rooted in family.
Built for progress.

Avanza ABA is an Arizona-based Center of Excellence for Autism, offering Applied Behavior Analysis therapy that is warm, individualized, and deeply family-centered. Our name — Spanish for "to advance" — reflects our mission: every individual, every family, moving forward.

Warm & Welcoming

A practice where every family feels seen, heard, and genuinely supported from day one.

Evidence-Based Practice

Every intervention grounded in Applied Behavior Analysis — proven, ethical, individualized.

Culturally Inclusive

Honoring every background, language, and family structure — because progress is universal.

Community Rooted

We are your neighbors — committed to Arizona families beyond the therapy room.

How It Works

Your path to progress

Getting started with Avanza ABA is simple. We guide you every step of the way — from your first call through active therapy and beyond.

1

Free Consultation

Connect with our team. We listen, answer questions, and learn about your family's goals — no pressure, no commitment.

2

Insurance Verification

We verify your benefits, explain coverage, and handle authorization — at no cost to you.

3

Behavioral Assessment

A BCBA conducts a comprehensive evaluation to understand your child's unique strengths and areas for growth.

4

Personalized Plan

We build an individualized treatment plan collaboratively with your family — with clear, measurable goals.

5

Therapy Begins

Sessions start in your preferred setting — home or school — with continuous progress monitoring.
